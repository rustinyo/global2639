About GlobalPixel

A free, privacy-first image editor built to run entirely in your browser — no uploads, no accounts, no cost.

Our Mission

GlobalPixel was created with a simple belief: everyone deserves powerful image editing tools without being forced to pay, register, or compromise their privacy. We built an editor that works 100% inside your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API, which means your images never leave your device.

Whether you need to resize a photo for a job application, compress an image for a website, or quickly convert between formats, GlobalPixel gives you the tools to do it instantly — with zero friction.

What You Can Do

GlobalPixel supports a full suite of image editing tools including crop, resize, rotate, flip, compress, convert (JPG, PNG, WEBP), and apply filters such as brightness, contrast, saturation, sepia, black & white, vivid, warm, cool, and invert. You can also pixelate images for creative or privacy purposes.

We support input formats including JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, BMP, and TIFF, and you can export your results in JPG, PNG, or WEBP.

Who We Are

GlobalPixel is an independent project dedicated to building fast, lightweight, and accessible web tools. We are a small team of developers passionate about open web technologies and user privacy. We do not collect personal data, sell advertising data, or share user information with third parties.

Our site is supported by non-intrusive display advertising (Google AdSense), which allows us to keep the service completely free for all users.
