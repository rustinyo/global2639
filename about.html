GlobalPixel About Us

Built for everyone who edits images

GlobalPixel is a free, privacy-first image editor that runs entirely inside your browser — no accounts, no uploads, no cost.

Our Mission

We believe powerful image editing tools should be accessible to everyone — designers, students, bloggers, small business owners, and everyday users alike. GlobalPixel was built to remove the barriers: no software to install, no account to create, no subscription to pay.

Everything happens locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images are never sent to any server, never stored, and never seen by anyone but you.

The Technology Behind GlobalPixel

GlobalPixel uses the browser's native Canvas API to perform all image operations — cropping, resizing, rotating, compressing, converting, and applying filters — without any server-side processing. This means your data stays private and results are instant.

We support input formats including JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, BMP, and TIFF, and can export to JPG, PNG, and WEBP at your chosen quality level.

Our Commitment to You

GlobalPixel will always be free. We are committed to keeping this tool open and accessible without paywalls or feature restrictions. Our goal is simple: give you a fast, reliable, and private image editor you can count on.
