Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here is exactly what we collect — and what we don't.

Last updated: June 2026

🔒 Your images are never uploaded to our servers. All editing happens locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. We cannot see, access, or store your files.

1. Information We Collect

GlobalPixel does not require registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or payment details.

We may collect limited, anonymous technical information through third-party services (such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense) including:

  • Your approximate geographic region (country/city level)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website

This data is aggregated and anonymized. It cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. Image Data

All image processing on GlobalPixel takes place entirely within your browser. When you upload an image:

  • It is read locally by your browser using the JavaScript FileReader API
  • All editing operations (crop, resize, compress, filter, etc.) are performed by the browser's HTML5 Canvas API
  • No image data is ever transmitted to our servers
  • When you close or refresh the page, all image data is immediately discarded

3. Cookies and Local Storage

GlobalPixel uses your browser's localStorage only to remember your language preference (e.g., if you switch to Turkish or Spanish). No personal data is stored in cookies or local storage.

Third-party services we use (Google AdSense, Google Analytics) may set their own cookies for advertising personalization and analytics purposes. You can manage or disable these through your browser settings or a cookie consent tool.

4. Advertising

GlobalPixel is free to use and is supported by display advertising provided by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense – for displaying advertisements
  • Google Fonts – for loading web fonts (DM Sans, Plus Jakarta Sans)
  • Google Analytics – for anonymous usage statistics (if enabled)

6. Children's Privacy

GlobalPixel does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Our service is designed for general audiences and does not include features targeted at children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page.
