FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GlobalPixel completely free?
Yes. GlobalPixel is 100% free with no premium plans, hidden fees, or watermarks — ever.
Do my images get uploaded to a server?
No. All processing happens entirely inside your browser using the Canvas API. Your images never leave your device.
What image formats are supported?
GlobalPixel supports JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, BMP, and TIFF as input, and can export to JPG, PNG, and WEBP.
Do I need to create an account?
No account or sign-up is required. Just open the editor, upload your image, and start editing instantly.
Does it work on mobile devices?
Yes, GlobalPixel is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops with any modern browser.
